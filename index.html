Welcome to Total STYLZ

Come join us on February 9th for the 2nd Annual Free Cuts for Kids 

Total Stylz is coming together with Amanda McCollum of Countryside Barbershop to give back to the community!

All donations will be split amongst two charities: Catch a Falling Star and Community Fellowship Youth Group.

Food and refreshments donated by Chop Shop Pizzeria.

What?

Free Cuts for Kids is an idea Katie and her husband came up with in 2017. They wanted to come up with a way to give back; all while raising mental health and suicide awareness.


Where?

Total STYLZ is located at 8403 RochesterRd Gasport, New York 14067. The event will run from 11-4pm on Feburary 9th.


Why?

This event is remember those who were lost and to embrace those who are experiencing it now. We want to remind everyone that there are options for help. Mental illness should not own you, so lets own it together.
