Appointments Only

Total Stylz does not have regular hours. Instead, the salon works off appointments only. However, that does not mean we will turn away a walk in. If you stop by, and we are in the salon, we might be able to squeeze you in after the clients with appointments. No promises a walk in can get a cut, but we will try our best! The safest way to get in style is by scheduling an appointment!
