Meet the Girls

Katie Finch

Hi everyone,

I am Katie Finch. A master stylist and the owner of Total STYLZ, a salon I opened about a year ago. It is located in Gasport, NY; which is the perfect spot for my salon, since I live right by in Middleport with my amazing husband and three kids. I have been in the hair business for over 15 years and I absolutely love it. To me, there is no better feeling than making my customers feel beautiful. I never shy away from adventures and am always willing to try different techniques on my customers. If you have a challenging hair problem, I say challenge accepted.  

Call for an appointment: 1(716)957-0905

Hi everyone,

I'm a country girl who loves all things "beauty". My greatest adventure is becoming a full-time wife and mother of two. I'm so thankful to be working in my community, just minutes away from home. The move to Total STYLZ has been a blessing!

I've been in the hair industry for over 15 years. Hi lighting and color are my specialty. I love the confidence a "fresh color" gives. I also enjoy Updo Styling for weddings and special occasions. The magic of bobby pins and hair spray is such a fun way to express my creative side! However, the best part about working behind the chair is meeting new people and making them feel good about themselves!
